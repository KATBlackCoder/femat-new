# Special Belt Promotion Ceremony - 4th, 5th and 6th Dan

**Bamako, December 6, 2025** – The Kwang Myung Centre in Bacodjicoroni ACI hosted an exceptional special belt promotion ceremony for 4th, 5th and 6th Dan belts on Saturday, December 6, 2025. Under the high patronage of the **Minister of Youth and Sports**, in charge of Civic Education and Citizen Building, Mr. **Abdoul Kassim Ibrahim Fomba**, this official ceremony was organized by the Malian Taekwondo Federation (FEMAT).

## A Ceremony Under the Sign of Excellence

The ceremony took place in a **friendly and fraternal atmosphere**, bringing together members of the Malian Taekwondo community, authorities and honored guests. It was marked by a **100% success rate**, with all candidates successfully validating their belt promotions, reflecting the quality of their preparation and commitment.

## Honorary Grades for High Authorities

On this memorable occasion, the Federation proceeded to award **honorary grades** to three high authorities of the Nation, recognizing their commitment to the development of Taekwondo and sport in Mali:

- **3rd Dan** awarded to **Minister of Youth and Sports**, Mr. Abdoul Kassim Ibrahim Fomba
- **4th Dan** attributed to **Prime Minister**, Major General Abdoulaye MAÏGA
- **5th Dan** awarded to **His Excellency Army General Assimi Goïta**, President of the Transition, Head of State
