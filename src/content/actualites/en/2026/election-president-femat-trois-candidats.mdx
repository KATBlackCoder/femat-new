# FEMAT Presidential Election Scheduled for January 31: Three Candidates Shortlisted

**Bamako, January 29, 2026** – On the eve of the elective general assembly for the presidency of the Malian Taekwondo Federation (FEMAT), scheduled for **Saturday, January 31, 2026** at the **Djoliba Centre in Bamako**, the application review commission has provided an update on the state of preparations and the shortlisted candidates.

## Three Candidates in the Running

There are **three candidates** in total — two men and one woman — shortlisted by the commission:

- **Dr. Alioune Badara Traoré**, outgoing president, seeking a new term;
- **Me Yacouba Oumar Samaké**;
- **Ms. Sy Aminata Makou Traoré**.

This information meeting, held on Thursday, January 29, 2026, was attended by commission members, Taekwondo practitioners and journalists.

## A First Election in Compliance with the Statutes

According to **Mamadou Diallo**, commission president, "Taekwondo is a family" and this is the **first time** that an election has been organized to choose the FEMAT president, in accordance with the federation's texts and regulations.

The commission was set up on **January 13, 2026**. Despite shortcomings noted in the applications of Ms. Sy Aminata Makou Traoré and Me Yacouba Oumar Samaké, the commission decided to retain all three applications for the benefit and cohesion of all Taekwondo practitioners, as emphasized by **Yacouba Traoré**, FEMAT Secretary General.

## A Major Gathering for Malian Taekwondo

Everything will be done to ensure that this elective general assembly takes place under good conditions, for the benefit of Malian Taekwondo. For many observers, this election promises a "duel of masters" for the federation's leadership.

**Who will be the new FEMAT president?** Verdict this Saturday, January 31, 2026 at the Djoliba Centre in Bamako.
