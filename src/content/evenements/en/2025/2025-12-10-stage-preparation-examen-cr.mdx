# Preparation Seminar - Red Belt Exam

Mandatory preparation seminar organized for all candidates for Red Belt promotion exams.

## Practical Information

### Dates and Times

- **Dates**: Wednesday 10 to Friday 12 December 2025
- **Time**: 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM each day
- **Venue**: Basketball Court, Centre Culturel et Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH), Bamako

### Participants

This seminar is **mandatory** for all promotion exam candidates:
- From 3rd Keup to 2nd Keup
- From 2nd Keup to 1st Keup
- Candidates already Red Belt 1st Keup

### Seminar Objectives

- Technical preparation for promotion exams
- Revision of techniques and forms (Poomsae)
- Physical and mental preparation
- Familiarization with exam requirements

## Important

**Registration of candidates for promotion exams is conditional on participation in this preparation seminar.**

Candidates must attend all seminar sessions to be allowed to sit the exam on Saturday 13 December 2025.
