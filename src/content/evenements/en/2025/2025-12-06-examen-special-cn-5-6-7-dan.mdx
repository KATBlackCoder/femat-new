# Special Black Belt 5th, 6th and 7th Dan Promotion Exam

The Malian Taekwondo Federation (FEMAT) is organizing a **special** promotion exam for **Black Belt 5th, 6th and 7th Dan** on **Saturday 6 December 2025** at Centre Kwang Myung, Bacodjicoroni ACI, from 8:00 AM.

This exam is organized by the **Promotion Commission** and the **Federal Bureau**.

## Grades Concerned

- **From 4th Dan to 5th Dan**
- **From 5th Dan to 6th Dan**
- **From 6th Dan to 7th Dan**

## Eligibility Conditions

1. **Exam fee payment**:
   - **85,000 FCFA** for 4th to 5th Dan candidates
   - **115,000 FCFA** for 5th to 6th Dan candidates
   - **165,000 FCFA** for 6th to 7th Dan candidates

2. **Be on the list of selected candidates** (see annex 4)

3. **Thesis presentation** on the theme in annex 2

4. **Video poomsae presentation**:
   - **5th Dan**: Gumgang, Taebaek, Pyeongwon, Shipjin (mandatory)
   - **6th Dan**: Taebaek, Pyeongwon, Shipjin, Jitae (mandatory)
   - See annex 3 for details

5. **Federal license presentation**: 2024 and 2025 at 3,000 FCFA each

6. **Federal passport presentation** or proof of payment

## Gathering

**Mandatory**: Candidate registration is conditional on participation in the gathering:

- **Date**: Friday 5 December 2025
- **Venue**: Centre Culturel Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH)
- **Time**: From 5:00 PM

## Registration Deadline

Exam fees, federal license and passport must reach the Permanent Secretariat at the federation headquarters, Stade Ouezzin Coulibaly, no later than **Thursday 4 December 2025 at 4:00 PM**.

**⚠️ Important**: No registration will be allowed on the day of the exam.

| GRADE | KUKKIWON PRICE (FCFA) |

## Thesis Outline

Candidates for 5th, 6th and 7th Dan must present a thesis according to the following outline:

1. **INTRODUCTION**
2. **PERSONAL PRESENTATION** (age, years of practice, evolution in Taekwondo, etc.)
3. **KNOWLEDGE OF MALIAN TAEKWONDO HISTORY**
4. **NATIONAL TAEKWONDO ORGANIZATION**
5. **EVOLUTION, ORGANIZATION AND PRINCIPLES OF WORLD TAEKWONDO**
6. **CONTRIBUTION TO MALIAN AND WORLD TAEKWONDO**
7. **IDEAS AND PROPOSALS FOR YOUR PLACE IN NATIONAL TAEKWONDO**
8. **CONCLUSION**
