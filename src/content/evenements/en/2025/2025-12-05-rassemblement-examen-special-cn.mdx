# Gathering - Special Black Belt 5th, 6th and 7th Dan Exam

Mandatory gathering organized for all candidates for the special **Black Belt 5th, 6th and 7th Dan** promotion exam.

## Practical Information

### Date and Time

- **Date**: Friday 5 December 2025
- **Time**: From 5:00 PM
- **Venue**: Centre Culturel Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH), Bamako

### Participants

This gathering is **mandatory** for all candidates for the special exam:
- Candidates for 4th to 5th Dan
- Candidates for 5th to 6th Dan
- Candidates for 6th to 7th Dan

## Important

**Registration of candidates for the special promotion exam is conditional on participation in this gathering.**

Candidates must attend the gathering to be allowed to sit the exam on Saturday 6 December 2025.
