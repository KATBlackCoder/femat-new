# Red Belt 2nd and 1st Keup Promotion Exam

The Malian Taekwondo Federation (FEMAT) is organizing the **2025 second semester** Red Belt promotion exams on **Saturday 13 December 2025** at Centre Kwang Myung, Bacodjicoroni ACI, from 8:00 AM.

## Grades Concerned

- **From 3rd Keup to 2nd Keup**
- **From 2nd Keup to 1st Keup**
- **Candidates already Red Belt 1st Keup**

## Eligibility Conditions

1. **Exam fee payment**:
   - 5,000 FCFA for 3rd to 2nd Keup candidates
   - 10,000 FCFA for 2nd to 1st Keup candidates
   - No seminar fee for those already Red Belt 1st Keup

2. **Seminar fee payment**: 3,000 FCFA per candidate (except Red Belt 1st Keup)

3. **Federal license presentation**: 2024 and 2025 at 2,000 FCFA each

4. **Federal passport presentation** or proof of payment

## Preparation Seminar

**Mandatory**: Candidate registration is conditional on participation in the seminar:

- **Dates**: Wednesday 10 to Friday 12 December 2025
- **Venue**: Basketball Court, Centre Culturel et Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH)
- **Time**: 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM

## Registration Deadline

Lists, exam fees, seminar fees and federal license fees from each club must reach the Permanent Secretariat at the federation headquarters, Stade Ouezzin Coulibaly, no later than **Wednesday 10 December 2025 at 4:00 PM**.

**⚠️ Important**: No registration will be allowed on the day of the exam.
