# Preparation Seminar - Black Belt Exam

Mandatory preparation seminar organized for all candidates holding **Red Belt 1st Keup and Black Belt** in preparation for Black Belt promotion exams.

## Practical Information

### Dates and Times

- **Dates**: Monday 15 to Friday 19 December 2025
- **Time**: 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM each day
- **Venue**: Basketball Court, Centre Culturel et Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH), Bamako

### Participants

This seminar is **mandatory** for:
- All Bamako District candidates
- Candidates holding **Red Belt 1st Keup**
- Candidates holding **Black Belt** (all grades)

### Seminar Objectives

- Technical preparation for promotion exams
- Revision of techniques and forms (Poomsae)
- Physical and mental preparation
- Familiarization with exam requirements

## Important

**Registration of Bamako District candidates for promotion exams is conditional on participation in this preparation seminar.**

Candidates must attend all seminar sessions to be allowed to sit the exam on Saturday 20 December 2025.
