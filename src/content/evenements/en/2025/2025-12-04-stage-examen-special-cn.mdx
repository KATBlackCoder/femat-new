# Preparation Seminar - Special Black Belt 5th, 6th and 7th Dan Exam

Mandatory preparation seminar organized for all candidates for the special **Black Belt 5th, 6th and 7th Dan** promotion exam.

## Practical Information

### Dates and Times

- **Dates**: Thursday 4 and Friday 5 December 2025
- **Time**: From 5:00 PM each day
- **Venue**: Centre Culturel Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH), Bamako

### Participants

This seminar is **mandatory** for all candidates for the special exam:
- Candidates for 4th to 5th Dan
- Candidates for 5th to 6th Dan
- Candidates for 6th to 7th Dan

### Seminar Objectives

- Technical preparation for promotion exams
- Revision of required poomsae (Gumgang, Taebaek, Pyeongwon, Shipjin, Jitae)
- Preparation for thesis presentation
- Physical and mental preparation
- Familiarization with special exam requirements

## Important

**Registration of candidates for the special promotion exam is conditional on participation in this preparation seminar.**

Candidates must attend all seminar sessions to be allowed to sit the exam on Saturday 6 December 2025.
