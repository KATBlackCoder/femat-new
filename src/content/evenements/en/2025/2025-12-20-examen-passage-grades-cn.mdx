# Black Belt 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th Dan Promotion Exam

The Malian Taekwondo Federation (FEMAT) is organizing **Black Belt 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th Dan** promotion exams on **Saturday 20 December 2025** at Centre Kwang Myung, Bacodjicoroni ACI, from 8:00 AM.

## Practical Information

### Date and Venue

- **Date**: Saturday 20 December 2025
- **Time**: From 8:00 AM
- **Venue**: Centre Kwang Myung, Bacodjicoroni ACI

### Registration Deadline

Candidate lists, exam fees, diploma and seminar fees must reach the Permanent Secretariat at the federation headquarters, Stade Ouezzin Coulibaly, no later than **Wednesday 17 December 2025 at 4:00 PM**, strictly.

**⚠️ Important**: No registration will be allowed on the day of the exam.

## Preparation Seminar

A preparation seminar will be organized for all candidates holding **Red Belt 1st Keup and Black Belt**:

- **Dates**: Monday 15 to Friday 19 December 2025
- **Venue**: Basketball Court, Centre Culturel et Islamique d'Hamdallaye (CCIH)
- **Time**: 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM
- **Mandatory**: For Bamako District candidates

## Seniority Requirements

### 2nd Dan

Candidates admitted to 1st Dan **two and a half (2.5) years or more ago** may sit the **Black Belt 2nd Dan** exam.

### 3rd Dan

Candidates admitted to 2nd Dan **three and a half (3.5) years or more ago** may sit the **Black Belt 3rd Dan** exam.

### 4th Dan

Candidates admitted to 3rd Dan in **2018 or earlier** may sit the **Black Belt 4th Dan** exam.
