# Application File for FEMAT Presidential Election

## Documents to Provide

Candidates must provide the following documents:

1. **A motivation letter** addressed to the Federation Secretary General;

2. **An updated CV** dated and signed;

3. **A certified copy of diplomas** and their equivalences if needed (at least a master's degree, training in sports management, knowledge of English will be an asset);

4. **A Malian nationality certificate**;

5. **A residence certificate** proving that the candidate has resided on Malian territory for two (02) years as of the date of the application;

6. **A criminal record extract** dated three (03) months or less;

7. **A copy of up-to-date Taekwondo documents** for practitioners (License, Sports passport, National or international Taekwondo diploma);

8. **Be up to date with statutory membership fees**.

## Eligibility Condition

To be eligible for the position of FEMAT president, any candidate must belong to one of the seven (07) main mother clubs (Article 2). A membership certificate must be provided for this purpose.

## Submission

**Application files must be submitted no later than January 24, 2026 at 12 noon at the FEMAT Secretariat, Ouezzin Coulibaly Stadium.**

## Electoral Process

Elections will be held in accordance with the statutes in force. The election will be limited to the president. The president will present a complete bureau by correspondence to the leagues within 30 days after the first bureau meeting.
